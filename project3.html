Question 2

The monetization reports tell what is being purchased most on the website. It informs the owner of the website what the audience is purchasing the most. It also relays information about the ad success on the website. The engagement reports will show which pages on the site have been accessed the most. The types of engagement reports are the events report, the conversion report and the pages and screen report. They will also show which types of user actions and errors occur on the site. The acquisition reports show how users land on the site. It also shows the effectiveness of marketing. The types of acquisition reports are user acquisition and traffic acquisition.
